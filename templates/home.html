About This Dataset

Metrics on this page are drawn from {{ summary.country_corridors | int | string }} bilateral corridors in the country-to-country extract — regional aggregates and income-group rollups are excluded to focus on direct country-pair flows.

The dataset covers {{ summary.snapshots }} time snapshots spaced roughly five years apart ({{ summary.time_span }}), allowing comparisons of how individual corridors have grown, shrunk, or emerged over three and a half decades.

Of the {{ summary.country_corridors | int | string }} corridors in the extract, {{ summary.active_corridors_2024 | int | string }} had measurable flows in 2024, meaning roughly {{ ((summary.active_corridors_2024 / summary.country_corridors) * 100) | round(0) | int }}% of all tracked pairs registered at least one migrant in the most recent snapshot.
